# Stay Safe Online -- Prevention Checklist

1.  **Never share your OTP or UPI PIN** with anyone, even if they claim
    to be from your bank.
2.  **Always verify UPI collect requests** before approving---receiving
    money does NOT require entering your PIN.
3.  **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on all important accounts
    like email, banking, and social media.
4.  **Avoid clicking on unknown links** received via SMS, WhatsApp, or
    email, especially those asking for personal details.
5.  **Use strong, unique passwords** for different accounts and update
    them regularly.
6.  **Download apps only from official stores** like Google Play Store
    or Apple App Store.
7.  **Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions**, as it may
    expose your data to attackers.
8.  **Log out from shared or public computers** after accessing your
    accounts.
9.  **Keep your devices updated** with the latest security patches and
    antivirus software.
10. **Be cautious of fake job offers, internships, or scholarships**
    that ask for payment or sensitive information.
